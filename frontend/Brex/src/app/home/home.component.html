COVID-19 infects the lung cells and an excess of inflammation liquid comes out. This inflammation liquid prevents O2 transfer to blood cells and patient starts to have breathing problems.

The current treatment is to perform breathing exercises to get rid of the inflammation liquid. The idea is to come with an app to perform breathing exercises to the user.

The app is going to check if the user is doing the breathing exercises right while detecting the expansion of the chest.
